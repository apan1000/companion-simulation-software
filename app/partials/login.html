Once upon a time there were some eggs.

These powerful beings were conceived through might and magic.

The eggs cracked open and war raged for centuries.

No one recalls who came out as winner in the end, but what we are sure of is that what came out of the eggs returned and has slumbered ever since.

After thousand years of slumbering the eggs have awoken once more.

Now it's your time to wield the power of the egg and fight the companions!
