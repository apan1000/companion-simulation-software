Caring

You will soon recieve your very own monster companion. You can take care of it by dragging items to it.

The Candy raises its level by one, the Bell increases happiness and the Potion increases HP.

The items are limited, so use them wisely!

Fighting

Fight wild monsters at the Fields to earn experience and items!
See who is the current master under Ranks. There you can also Challenge people in an online duel or Practice with their companion in an offline battle.
If your monster is defeated, it will fade away, so be careful!

The Bar


Walk around with your monster and interact with other players at the Bar!
